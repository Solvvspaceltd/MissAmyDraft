What Miss Amy offers

Miss Amy provides specialized professional development and mentoring for early years practitioners, childcare professionals and educators looking to deepen their knowledge and improve their practice. With 10+ years experience, EYFS certification, and specialist expertise in SEN support, Miss Amy works with individuals and small teams to build confidence and competence.

EYFS Framework Mentoring

Personalized guidance on implementing EYFS effectively, assessment methods, and best practice in early childhood education.

  • 1-to-1 mentoring sessions
  • Curriculum planning support
  • Assessment & observation techniques
  • Parent partnership strategies

SEN & Inclusion Training

Specialist coaching on supporting children with additional needs, non-verbal communication, and creating genuinely inclusive environments.

  • SEN identification & support planning
  • Communication strategies
  • Sensory-aware practice
  • Working with external professionals

Professional Development Workshops

Bespoke training sessions for your team on topics including child development, safeguarding practice, and behaviour management.

  • Custom workshop design
  • Small group sessions
  • Interactive & practical
  • Follow-up support included
