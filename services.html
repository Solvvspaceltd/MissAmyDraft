  • Sleep & feeding routines followed
  • Sensory & exploratory play
  • Daily diary & parent updates

EYFS aligned learning for pre-school age children, focused on school readiness, phonics, numeracy and social development.

Before and after school care for primary-age children. Safe, nurturing and fun helping working families manage the school day with ease.
