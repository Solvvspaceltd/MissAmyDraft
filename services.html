Childcare tailored to your family

Flexible sessions designed around working families, with full-day, part-time, wraparound and specialist care options available.

Baby & Toddler Care

Dedicated care for children aged 3 months to 3 years. Structured routines, sensory play and gentle early learning in a home-from-home environment.

  • Key person approach
  • Sleep & feeding routines followed
  • Sensory & exploratory play
  • Daily diary & parent updates
Price on application — contact us to discuss

Early Years Programme (3–5 yrs)

EYFS-aligned learning for pre-school age children, focused on school readiness, phonics, numeracy and social development.

  • EYFS framework delivery
  • School readiness focus
  • Phonics & early literacy
  • Government funded hours accepted
Price on application — funded hours may apply

Wraparound Care

Before and after school care for primary-age children. Safe, nurturing and fun — helping working families manage the school day with ease.

  • Morning drop from 7:30am
  • After school collection available
  • Homework support offered
  • Hot snack provided
Price on application / per session

Holiday Club

Exciting, theme-based activity programmes during school holidays. Trips, crafts, outdoor learning and themed weeks that children look forward to.

  • Themed weekly activities
  • Local trips & outings
  • Outdoor learning & sports
  • Arts, crafts & cooking
Price on application / per week or day

Government-funded childcare hours

Miss Amy's Daycare is registered to accept 15 and 30 hours of government-funded childcare for eligible families. We also accept childcare vouchers. Contact us to check your eligibility and available hours.
