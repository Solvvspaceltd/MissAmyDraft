Miss Amy — Lead Childminder & EYFS Practitioner

Surrey Quays, SE16 · Ofsted Registered · 10+ Years Experience
Her Story

Dedicated to early childhood from day one

Miss Amy founded her daycare setting in Surrey Quays with a clear vision: to create a childcare environment that felt like a genuine extension of home — warm, safe, stimulating and deeply caring.

With over a decade of experience in early childhood education and care, Miss Amy brings a wealth of professional knowledge and an innate nurturing instinct to everything she does. She is a qualified EYFS practitioner, paediatric first aider, and trained safeguarding lead.

One of Miss Amy's greatest strengths — and something parents comment on time and again — is her ability to connect with and support children with additional needs. Her specialist experience with non-verbal children and those with limited speech has helped many families find a setting where their child truly belongs.

Miss Amy's long-term vision is exciting: to grow from a highly regarded childminding setting into a fully registered early years prep school — raising the standard of early education in the Surrey Quays community.
