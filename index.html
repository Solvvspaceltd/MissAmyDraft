About Miss Amy

A trusted name in Surrey Quays childcare

Miss Amy's Daycare is a professional, Ofsted-registered early years setting based in Surrey Quays, SE16. With over a decade of experience in early childhood education, Miss Amy provides a safe, stimulating, and deeply nurturing environment where every child is seen, valued, and supported.

Rooted in the EYFS framework, the setting places a strong emphasis on school readiness, emotional wellbeing, and inclusive practice — including specialist experience supporting children with limited speech and non-verbal communication needs.

Serving families across Southwark and the surrounding communities, Miss Amy's Daycare is committed to building strong partnerships with parents and carers at every stage of their child's early journey.

Daily Routine

A day at Miss Amy's Daycare

Every day is structured, nurturing and full of purposeful learning experiences.

7:30am

Morning welcome & free play

Children arrive, settle in and choose from a range of open-ended play activities and resources.

9:00am

Circle time & group learning

Songs, stories, phonics and early maths in a group setting — building language and listening skills.

10:30am

Outdoor play & physical development

Garden exploration, gross motor activities and nature-based learning in the fresh air.

12:00pm

Lunch & social skills

Healthy, balanced meals encouraging independence, conversation and positive mealtimes.

1:00pm

Rest time / quiet activities

Nap time for younger children; quiet reading, drawing or puzzles for older ones.

3:00pm

Creative & sensory activities

Arts, crafts, messy play, sensory trays and themed creative projects tied to the curriculum.

5:30pm

Home time

End-of-day handover with parents including verbal updates and daily diary entries.
